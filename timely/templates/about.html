About Us

A task manager that actually saves you time.


Timely was started by a group of four Princeton computer science majors from the Class of 2022. We were tasked with creating an application for our COS333 class under the guidance of Professor Bob Dondero and our TA Nick Turner.

We noticed that homework managers were ubiquitous among students, but that they usually took up more time than they saved since inputting tasks took so long. Additionally, we wanted to combine usual homework management features like completion tracking with more advanced analytics like time taken on an assignment to provide useful insights about student work habits that could be used to manage time better.

"You can't manage what you can't measure."
